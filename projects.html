Pretty Shop - Your One-Stop Shop for All Things Beautiful

Stylish Design: Clean, modern, and intuitive.
Responsive Layout: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile.
Product Grid: Browse featured products with images, names, prices, and 'Add to Cart' buttons.
Interactive Cart: Dynamic updates as you add products, showing item details and total price.

Technologies Used:
HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript
